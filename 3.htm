Strands



Science Technology Engineering and Mathematics(STEM)offers students how to apply math, science, engineering, and technology to solve real-world problems, fostering critical thinking and collaboration skills. Graduates can pursue high-paying careers in fields like software development, engineering, and robotics.



Humanities And Social Sciences(HUMSS)allows students to investigate human behavior, societal issues, and culture, providing profound knowledge in politics, literature, arts, and society.

The General Academic Strand (GAS) helps students develop critical thinking skills, explore different ideas, and form their own opinions. It also helps students find their interests and passions.

TVL-ICT prepares students for a career in Information and Communication Technology. It equips you with advanced skills in computer systems, programming, creating web pages, and basic animation.

TVL-EIM hones the skills of the students to repair and installation of domestic refrigeration and air-conditioning servicing, transmission line installation and maintenance, electrical installation and maintenance.
